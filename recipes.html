Manti

Traditional dumplings filled with meat and onions, popular in Central Asia.

  • Flour
  • Water

  • Salt and pepper

Beshbarmak

A traditional dish with boiled meat served over noodles.

Ingredients

  • Beef or lamb
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Onions
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil meat until tender.
  2. Prepare dough and cut into noodles.
  3. Cook noodles in broth.
  4. Slice meat.
  5. Serve meat over noodles with onions.

Rice with Chicken

A simple and tasty meal combining rice and seasoned chicken.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook chicken with vegetables.
  2. Add rice and water.
  3. Season with spices.
  4. Simmer until rice is ready.
  5. Serve hot.

Meat in French Style

Baked meat topped with potatoes, cheese, and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • Pork or beef
  • Potatoes
  • Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Slice meat and potatoes.
  2. Layer ingredients in a baking dish.
  3. Add mayonnaise and cheese.
  4. Bake in oven.
  5. Serve warm.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Classic Italian pasta with eggs, cheese, and bacon.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Bacon
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti.
  2. Fry bacon.
  3. Mix eggs and cheese.
  4. Combine all ingredients.
  5. Serve immediately.
